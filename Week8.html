Week 8

This week we are piecing together the elements in Adabox 002 and creating a robot that can be controlled via bluetooth. The robot can be accessed by an application on the phone and we changed the arduino code to make the robot sing a different song from its original one.

Our intention was to make the robot sing the theme song of Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, but the score is too long and we did not have enough range defined on the Piezo element, so the end result is this.
