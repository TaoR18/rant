Week 9/10

This week we are to make our own personalized skateboards!

My design is centered around the band Nirvana. I try to couple the R and the A in Nirvana with Reserve Scope Logo and an ancient club logo for Arsenal.

The design is here.

After I successfully glued the board together and vacuum formed the shape, I spray painted the entire board black as the background color and tried to create a sticker to stick onto the skateboard.

The file is here.

However, as I stick the sticker onto the board I realized that the black on the board and the black on the sticker are two different colors. Thus I tried again with nor transparent stickers but stickers with a white background.

Finally I used the laser cutter to create a mask for spray paint.

And the results are fine, except for some rough border lines that I refined with regular tempera paint.





The file for laser cutting is here.
