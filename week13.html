Week 13

This week we were asked to practice our skills with the Arduino kit. We need to complete 6 projects from the guide and modify the code of one of them.

For the guide projects, I did the Circuit 1, 2, 6, 8, 11, 13.

Circuit 1 is just LED blinking.

Circuit 2 is multiple LEDs exhibiting a pattern.

Circuit 6 incorporates the Piezo element and plays a tune.

Circuit 8 uses the reading from the potentiometer to determine how fast the LED blinks.

Circuit 11 uses a relay as an on-off switch to control the LED to blink.

Circuit 13 reads in the pressure from the pressure sensor to determine how bright the LED should light up.

For the modified circuit, I slightly modified the code in Circuit 13 so that when a person presses down, the LED turns off; otherwises it stays on.
